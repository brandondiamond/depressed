NYC is depressed.

A community-organized movement to address depression in nyc.

Depression affects nearly 10% of New Yorkers. Most do not receive treatment— largely due to the isolation and embarassment caused by stigma.

We are an informal, grassroots organization of survivors and allies committed to ending the stigma, once and for all.

Our goals.

  • To fight stigma through visibility.
  • To provide accurate information about depression.
  • To share stories that promote empathy, unity, and awareness.
