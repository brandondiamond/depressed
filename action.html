1. Become visible

Wear a black bracelet to remind people living with depression that they're not alone. Then, post a photo with the #depressednyc hashtag.

Make your own with string or a hair tie. Or, pick up one of our shirts (we're strictly not-for-profit).

We use social media to inform people about depression in NYC. By following and sharing, you can help us reach more people.
